Overview

1. Electrical Engineering is a field of engineering that deals with the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism. 2. It involves the design, development, and maintenance of electrical systems, devices, and equipment.

Subfields

1. Power Engineering: Deals with generation, transmission, and distribution of electric power.
2. Control Systems: Focuses on automation and control of electrical systems.
3. Electronics: Concerned with the design and development of electronic circuits and devices.
4. Communication Engineering: Involves transmission and reception of information through electrical signals.
5. Computer Engineering: Combines electrical engineering and computer science to develop computer hardware and software.
6. Microelectronics: Deals with the design and development of microelectronic devices and circuits.
