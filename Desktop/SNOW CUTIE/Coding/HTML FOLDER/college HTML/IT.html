Information technology (IT) is a field that deals with the collection, storage, protection, and exchange of data, as well as the development of the hardware and software needed to do so. It's an important part of business and commerce today. Here are some areas of IT:

Cybersecurity

The use of technologies, processes, and practices to protect networks, computers, programs, and data from unauthorized access, damage, or attack.

Networking

The technology that allows data to be transferred between devices on a network and communication between networks.

Cloud computing

A concept that plays a vital role in data storage and processing, and is also important for the Internet of Things (IoT).

Analytics

A branch of IT that deals with automating machine capabilities, such as teaching machines core languages.

Automation

An emerging discipline that uses computers and IT to manufacture equipment and machines without manual operation or with very little manual operation.
